// About the Artist

Dark Art.
Human Hands.
Zero AI.

Banana Artworks is the work of a German expat living in Chiang Rai, Thailand. Each piece in the Genesis Collection is a unique original — painted by hand, then digitally processed into something darker and stranger.

In a world flooded with AI-generated imagery, these are artifacts of actual human expression. The chaos, the imperfection, the obsession — it's all real.

"Every skull has a story.
Every banana carries a warning.
These 101 works are not generated —
they are made. By hand. With intention.
From the dark side of the tropics."

— Banana Artworks, Chiang Rai 2024
